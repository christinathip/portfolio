My Background

My name is Christina Thiphavong, "CT".

I'm originally from Providence, Rhode Island. Eventually my parents moved my brother and I to Murfreesboro, Tennessee. Which is a city outside of Nashville, Tennessee, "Music City."

I attended Middle Tennesse State University, majoring in Computer Science and Aerospace with the concentration in Unmanned Aircraft Systems(UAS),

Job Experience: 2016. Student Intern - Computer Science Nissan.

Recently I decided to take a break from college and wanted a new scenery.

Which is why I moved to Seattle and why I am enrolled in Epicodus.

I picked Seattle because I believe there's more opportunities for this small town girl here. Especially since I want to pursue a career in the tech industry. I love creating and being innovated which is why I want a career in the tech industry.

Hopefully, with the help of Epicodus, I'll be able to reach my goal of becoming a web-developer.
